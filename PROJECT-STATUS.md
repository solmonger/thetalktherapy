## Deployment Options Research

### Free Tier Options:
1. **GitHub Pages**
   - Pros: Free, integrates with GitHub, simple static hosting
   - Cons: Limited to static sites, no server-side processing

2. **Vercel**
   - Pros: Excellent free tier, automatic deployments, great performance
   - Cons: May have limits on bandwidth/build minutes

3. **Netlify**
   - Pros: Similar to Vercel, good free tier, forms handling
   - Cons: Similar limitations as Vercel

4. **Cloudflare Pages**
   - Pros: Fast, good security, generous free tier
   - Cons: Less mature ecosystem than Vercel/Netlify

### Recommended Approach:
**GitHub Pages + Vercel** for redundancy and flexibility.

## Technical Stack Considerations
