Color Usage Guidelines

Soft Peach (#FFD8C8)

Primary CTAs, highlights, important buttons. Use to draw attention to key actions.

Soft Sage (#C8D8C8)

Backgrounds, section dividers, cards. Creates calming separation between content areas.

Cream (#FFF8F0)

Main page background. Provides warm, inviting base for all content.

Warm Gray (#E8E0D8)

Borders, subtle elements, disabled states. Adds depth without harsh contrast.

Website Design Concepts

Three distinct approaches for The Talk Therapy website:

Concept A: Calm Sanctuary

Full-width hero with soft gradient, card-based content sections, rounded corners throughout, subtle animations. Focus on creating a peaceful, contained space.

Concept B: Conversation Flow

Asymmetrical layout, organic flowing shapes, dialogue-inspired elements. Mimics the natural flow of therapeutic conversation.

Concept C: Minimal Comfort

Ultra-clean layout, maximum whitespace, focus on typography, subtle texture backgrounds. Lets the content breathe and speak for itself.
